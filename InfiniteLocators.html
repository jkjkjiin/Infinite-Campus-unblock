How This Works
When You Browse The Internet, Often Times You Will Come Across
A Website With A Server Connected To It.
This Could Be For A Variety Of Reasons, But For This Website,
Its The Live Discord Chat, And The Movie Streamer
The Way Your Device Connects To These Servers Is That It Sends A Network Request
To The Server. These Network Requests Usually Contain: The Local IP (Yours), Your Browser,
And Some Other Info. These Can Be Used To Find Your IP, State, And City Although IPV4 Addresses
Cannot Accurately Locate Someone.
